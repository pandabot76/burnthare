Privacy Policy

**Burnt Hare Events** (referred to as "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors and race participants. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

**Last Updated:** September 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information primarily to facilitate race entry, provide event services, and improve our website experience.

Information you provide directly:

  • **Race Entry Data:** Name, date of birth, gender, emergency contact details, medical information (for safety purposes), email address, and postal address.
  • **Communication Data:** Information you provide when contacting us via email or forms.
  • **Marketing Preferences:** Whether you consent to receive marketing updates from us.

Information collected automatically:

  • **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • **Usage Data (Analytics):** Information about how you use our website, such as pages viewed, time spent on pages, and referring sites.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • **Event Management:** To process your race entry, assign race numbers, share results, and manage timing.
  • **Safety:** To provide emergency services with necessary medical and contact details in case of an incident.
  • **Communication:** To send essential pre-race instructions, updates, and post-race follow-ups.
  • **Marketing:** To send promotional emails about future Burnt Hare events, but only where consent has been given.
  • **Website Improvement:** To analyze site usage to identify popular content and improve user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share your personal data with third parties necessary to run our events and services, or when legally required.

  • **Booking Partners:** Your entry data is shared with our secure third-party race registration system (e.g., Let's Do This) for payment processing and entry management.
  • **Timing Contractors:** Necessary data (name, race number, category) is shared with timing companies to provide accurate results.
  • **Emergency Services:** Emergency contact and medical information is made available to race medics and emergency personnel.
  • **Legal Obligations:** We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use **cookies** to help us understand how you use our website and remember your preferences (like your choice of **Light or Dark Mode**).

  • **Essential Cookies:** Required for basic site function (e.g., maintaining your theme preference).
  • **Analytical/Performance Cookies:** Used to count visitors and see how they move around the site (e.g., via Google Analytics).

You can usually set your browser to remove or reject cookies. Please be aware that if you choose to do so, some parts of the website may not function properly.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), you have rights regarding your personal data:

  • **Access:** You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • **Rectification:** You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • **Erasure:** You have the right to request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations (e.g., retaining results data).
